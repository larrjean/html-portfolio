TOP MOVIES FOR LARRAINE

My top 3 movies as of date and WHY

1. Shutter Island

The best psychological thriller movie that had me questioning the ending for years. Until I just accepted that's how the director wanted it to be.

2. Argo

This movie had me shook and on my feet from beginning to end. Can't predict what could happen next. And I was amazed how the director and the lead actor is one and the same.

3. Miracle in Cell No. 7

Painful, sad story involving a mentally challenged father trying to care for her daughter. It may have started that way but the end was vindicating.
